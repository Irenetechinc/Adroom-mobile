# AdRoom Branding Concepts

## 1. Neon Neural Network (Selected Theme)
*   **Visual Style**: Dark mode, Cyberpunk aesthetics.
*   **Colors**: Deep Space Black (#0B0F19), Electric Cyan (#00F0FF), Neon Purple (#7000FF).
*   **Logo Concept**: A stylized letter "A" formed by connected glowing nodes (representing the neural network) against a dark void background. The nodes pulse slightly.
*   **Typography**: Monospaced or geometric sans-serif (e.g., Orbitron, Rajdhani).
*   **Icon**: A single glowing hexagon with the "A" node structure inside.

## 2. The Digital Prism
*   **Visual Style**: Glassmorphism, Clean, Futuristic White/Silver.
*   **Colors**: White, Silver, Holographic Gradients.
*   **Logo Concept**: A refracted light beam passing through a prism, creating a spectrum that forms the shape of a room or box.
*   **Icon**: A crystal-like cube with internal light reflections.

## 3. The Pulse
*   **Visual Style**: Minimalist, Data-driven.
*   **Colors**: Navy Blue, Bright Green (Data stream).
*   **Logo Concept**: A minimal waveform or frequency line that loops to form a speech bubble (representing Chat/Agent).
*   **Icon**: A green waveform circle on a navy background.

## 4. Orbital Intelligence
*   **Visual Style**: Space, Sci-Fi.
*   **Colors**: Deep Blue, Gold, White.
*   **Logo Concept**: A central core (AI) surrounded by orbiting rings (Campaigns/Strategies).
*   **Icon**: An atomic structure with a glowing core.

## 5. Matrix Flow
*   **Visual Style**: Hacker/Coder aesthetic, Terminal green.
*   **Colors**: Black, Terminal Green.
*   **Logo Concept**: Cascading digital rain forming the silhouette of a marketing funnel.
*   **Icon**: A binary code block with a highlighted "AR" (AdRoom).
