Frequently Asked

Quick answers

The questions our customers ask most often, answered in plain language.

What exactly does AdRoom AI do for me?
AdRoom AI is an autonomous marketing workforce. After you connect your ad accounts, the AI plans, launches, monitors, and optimizes campaigns 24/7 across Meta, TikTok, Google, and other platforms — without you having to touch the dashboard. You set the goal; the AI does the work.
Do I need ad spend to use AdRoom AI?
Yes — paid platforms still require their own ad budget that you control through your platform billing. Energy credits cover the AI's work (planning, optimization, creative generation, monitoring). Your media spend is paid directly to Meta, Google, etc., not to us.
What are energy credits?
Energy credits are the unit of work for the AI. Each plan includes a monthly allowance, and you can top up at any time. Heavy operations (creative generation, deep research, long strategy runs) cost more credits than lightweight monitoring.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel from Settings → Energy → Subscription. Your access continues through the end of the current billing cycle, and your data stays with you.
How do you protect my data?
All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest. Authentication tokens are stored in hardware-backed secure storage on your device. We never sell or share your campaign data, and you can request a full export or deletion at any time from Privacy & Security.
