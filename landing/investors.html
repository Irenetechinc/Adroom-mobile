Frequently Asked

Investor FAQ

Why the same price as the main round?

You're getting early access. You're not getting a discount — you're getting the opportunity to invest before the round opens to everyone, with a lower minimum.

What if the main round valuation goes higher?

We are committed to ₦1B for 10% at the $5M valuation. If terms change, early investors will receive the same or better terms — never worse.

What if the main round never happens?

You receive a full refund of your investment after 18 months. Your capital is protected.

How do I know my investment is safe?

We provide a formal convertible note agreement, monthly investor updates, and full transparency. Patents pending. Product complete and live.

Can I invest more than ₦155 million?

No. ₦155M is the maximum any single investor can put in, since that already represents 2% of the entire company — the hard cap for the early window.

When do I receive my shares?

Shares are issued automatically when the main pre-seed round closes — projected in 6 to 12 months from today.

What is the absolute minimum?

₦1,000,000 — One Million Naira. This is the lowest entry point we offer.

What if I want my money back before an IPO or acquisition?

You're protected by the 24-Month Exit Guarantee. Any time after Month 24, you can request a full buyback of your shares at the company's then-current valuation — no questions asked. The amount you receive reflects the company's market value at that time, so if the company has grown 2× or more, you naturally receive 2× or more of what you put in.

How does the 24-month exit actually work?

Starting on the first day of Month 25, you may submit a written buyback request. AdRoom AI is contractually obligated to repurchase your shares at the then-current per-share valuation (based on the most recent priced round or independent valuation). Payment is made in full within 90 days of the request. There is no guaranteed multiple — the buyback simply reflects fair market value at the time of your exit.
