What happens next?

Our engineering team treats bug reports as priority signal.

1. We reproduce it

An engineer will try to reproduce the issue using the steps you provided, usually within 24 hours.

2. We fix it

Critical bugs ship within 24–48 hours. Lower-severity fixes go out in the next regular release.

3. We email you

You'll get a personal note when the fix is live, so you can confirm it's resolved on your end.
